EXPERIENCE

Brief History

Our first office was opened 10 years ago. Throughout our history, we've worked hard to provide you with maximum comfort when traveling and moving around the city.
2010
Our CEO came up with the idea of creating a rental company with budget cars. We started with two used cars that were kept in the founder’s garage.
2012
The first expansion of FASTCAR took place. Customers were interested in renting cars, so we started to buy used cars in good condition and expanded the fleet to 15 cars.
2015
We opened new rental segments - business class and SUVs. We bought 5 new cars for each segment. Jaguar XJ, Ford Mustang GT, BMW 7-series joined our fleet.
2019
We completely reoriented to premium car rental. A segment of sports cars was added, we bought 4 sports cars including Ferrari 430 Italia and Aston Martin Valkyrie.

FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

We offer simple and transparent car rental conditions for any period. Look through the information below, and if you have any questions, we will be happy to answer them.
How can I rent a car with your service?
You should come to our office or call us. Our specialist will help with the selection and make out the documents.
How long does it take to pick up the car?
You will have to wait no more than 20 minutes, and then you'll be able to pick up the car wherever you want.
What is the minimum rental period?
You can rent a car even for 1 day. The maximum period is not provided. We offer discounts for 10+ days of rental.
What if I want to extend the rental period?
Just call our manager and ask to extend the rental period. The total amount will be charged from the card automatically.
How do I pay for the rental period?
Before you pick up the car, specify your credit card details. When you return the car, we charge the total amount.
Do you have any age or other restrictions?
Each driver should have a valid driver‘s license for the duration of the rental period and be at least age 18.
